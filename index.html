James Gosling

1- Education Life

James Gosling was born in May 1955 in Canada. He went university of Calgary to study computer science and he got BSc from here in 1977. After that university he started Carnegie-Mellon University in Pittsburgh. He also took a PhD from Carnegie-Mellon University and graduated in 1983. While doing his doctorate he wrote Gosling Emacs which is version of Emacs.

2- Career Life

He was interested almost all kind of softwares such as ground analysis, ISIS 2 satellite and of course Unix Emacs... Previous to join Sun Microsystems, multi-processor version of Unix was built by Gosling. Then, he joined Sun Microsystems. He had indescribable years at Sun Microsystems where he worked as a VP & Fellow. While working there people called him “Father of the java” cause he is the unique developer of the java programming language. He created java programming language, which is now one of the fastest and making progress devices for the web, at Sun. In the beginning his work was leader of the NeWS window system. He spent years there and while at Sun M. he built several compilers, mail systems, window managers and a WYSIWYG text editor. He stayed Sun Microsystems until 2 April 2010. Low of pay, decision skill, chancing of role, status and ethical challenges were his mention about why he left. He worked momentarily for Oracle with experiences that he gained from Sun. After that he worked at Google. After leaving Google he joined Liquid Robotics, which makes data collecting ocean robots, as their leader Software Architect.
